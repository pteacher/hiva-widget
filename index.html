Admissions and Programs

Passing score

The passing score required for university admission at Ala-Too International University is one hundred and ten (110) points for the main test.

Internal tests

Ala-Too International University conducts an internal test in English to determine the level of language proficiency.

University transfers

Students can transfer, but with the exception of those who are in their first and last year at their current university. Additionally, you would typically need to provide an academic transcript from your current institution when applying for a transfer.

Tuition and Discounts

.

International Olympiad Winners
Students who achieve top positions (1st, 2nd, or 3rd) in International Olympiads (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, Informatics) can avail a 100% discount. They have the privilege to apply to any department within the university.
National Level University Entrance Exam (GRТ) Discounts
  • Scoring 219-240 points grants a 100% discount at any department.
  • Scoring 210-218 points leads to a 75% discount.
  • Scoring 200-209 points qualifies for a 50% discount.
  • Scoring 175-199 points results in a 25% discount.
  • Scoring 156-174 points allows for a 10% discount.
  • Scoring 140-155 points offers a 5% discount.
Special Achievements
  • Graduates with a Diploma with honors receive a 20% discount.
  • AIU college graduates with a Diploma with honors receive a 25% discount.
  • Holders of Certificate with honors of basic general education (9th grade) can study at AIU college with a 10% discount.
  • "Sapat" high school graduates receive a 20% discount.
  • 1st place winners in the 11th grade "Robotics" competition organized by IEO "Sapat" are eligible for a 20% discount.
Special Circumstances
  • Students whose parents have passed away can avail a 30% discount upon documentation.
  • Students without a mother or father can receive a 10% discount upon documentation.
  • A 10% discount is offered to students from families with 4 children and aged below 22.
Notes

Students are eligible for only one discount. Discounts are subject to certain conditions and requirements. A 4 academic debt limit exists; exceeding it results in the automatic expiration of the discount. Discounts are not renewed after clearing academic debts in subsequent semesters.

College and Specialties

  • 1996

    Faculty of Engineering and Computer Science

    The Faculty offers a range of innovative and comprehensive programs to prepare students for exciting careers in technology and computing. Our faculty is committed to providing high-quality education and fostering a strong foundation in engineering and computer science disciplines.

  • 1996

    Faculty of Social Sciences

    Faculty offers programs in several areas, including Linguistics, Pedagogy, Philology, Psychology, International Law, and Journalism. These programs emphasize communication skills, cultural awareness, and analytical thinking, enabling students to engage meaningfully in the humanities and related disciplines

  • 2000

    Faculty of Economics and Administrative Sciences

    Faculty offers programs in various disciplines, including International Economics and Business, Finance and Credit, International relationships, Management. These programs are tailored to prepare students for successful careers in business, economics, and administrative roles.

  • 2013

    IT and Business College

    Our college provides a range of programs, including Computer Science and Automation Systems Software, Economics and Accounting, Marketing, and Computer Systems and Complexes. These programs are designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in their chosen fields

  • 2016

    Faculty of Medicine

    faculty offers programs in two main directions: General Medicine and Pediatrics. These programs provide students with a strong foundation in medical sciences, clinical practice, and patient care, ensuring that graduates are well-equipped to pursue successful careers in healthcare. We provide state-of-the-art laboratories, simulation centers, and clinical training opportunities that allow students to develop practical skills and gain real-world experience in a controlled environment. These facilities are designed to mimic real medical settings and enhance students' learning experience.

Benefits of Admission to Our University

There are discounts of up to 100% for good results in the National Level University Entrance Exam. A 20% discount is provided for the Certificate with Honors.

Discounts for Applicants

Various discounts are available for applicants based on achievements and circumstances. These include discounts for Olympiad winners, high school graduates, and those with special family situations. Students can receive discounts ranging from 5% to 100%.

Tuition for Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science and Engineering

The tuition fee for the Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering is $2,500 per year.

Tuition for Master's Program in Pedagogy

The tuition fee for the Master's program in Pedagogy is $1,200 per year.
